NELS 50 Proceedings Guidelines • January 2020

Dear Author:

As we have every year since 1981, GLSA Publications will be publishing the proceedings of NELS 50. We are now ready to accept submissions.

Style Sheets and Deadline

The deadline for submissions is May 8th, 2020, 11:59PM EST.
Please let us know by January 31st whether you are planning to submit.

The maximum lengths for submissions are defined as follows:
Invited talk: 20 pages,
Paper presentation (including special sessions): 14 pages,
Talk alternate: 10 pages,
Poster: 10 pages.

You will find the style sheets for LaTeX and Microsoft Word below. We really do encourage the use of LaTeX. Word users, please note that we will not be accepting any file format other than '.docx'.

• LaTeX users (.zip)
• Word users (.docx)
Instructions
LaTeX users
After extracting the contents of 'nels50latex.zip' you will find several files. The file 'nels50style.tex' was used to compile the PDF version of the styleguide and 'nels50style.bib' is the corresponding BibTeX file. Modify and use them as templates for your submission. The remaining document class (.cls) and bibliography style (.bst) files should not be modified but appropriately mentioned within your .tex file.

Word users
Please modify and use the 'nels50style.docx' as a template for your submission.

To submit, attach the source file and a PDF version of your document, as well as all files necessary to compile it (.bib file, figures, etc.) and send to nelseditors@gmail.com.

As always, we look forward to hearing from you. If you have questions or need further information, feel free to send us a new email at nelseditors@gmail.com with the subject line "NELS 50 - [last name]". It is our goal to help make this process as smooth as possible for you.
